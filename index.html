About me

I’m a Robotics M.S. student at Purdue University with a mechatronics background. I build and validate autonomous systems end-to-end—from motion planning and control to sensor integration, perception, and troubleshooting on hardware.

I’ve worked with ROS2, Gazebo/RViz, LiDAR, depth cameras, IMUs, sensor fusion (EKF), and vision pipelines (OpenCV). I enjoy turning real-world constraints into reliable robot behavior through careful testing and clean system integration.

  • Controls & Embedded

    PID/LQR/MPC prototyping, embedded development (Jetson, Raspberry Pi, Arduino/Teensy).
